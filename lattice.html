Lattice

The house evolves from the contextual climatic conditions which helps the house breath and respond in its semi urban setting

Project Details

Project: Lattice

Location : Trissur , India

The entry highlights the rubble wall creating a space of interest in the beginning itself. The wall is continued throughout the inner courtyard. The rubble wall enclosure is locally quarried granite that provides the interior spaces thermal comfort, when weathered over time camouflage itself with the surrounding. The random rubble masonry wall along the patio looks like a compound wall, acting as privacy. The surface is kept natural and its texture was kept rough.
